Fluffy Protein Pancakes

380
CALORIES
32g
PROTEIN
35g
CARBS
10g
FAT

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix 1 scoop vanilla protein powder, 1/2 cup oat flour, 1 tsp baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk 1 egg, 1/2 cup almond milk, and 1 tsp vanilla extract.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients, stirring until just mixed (small lumps are okay).
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly coat with cooking spray.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake and cook until bubbles form on top (about 2-3 minutes).
  6. Flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Serve with sugar-free syrup and fresh berries.

Greek Chicken Power Bowl

420
CALORIES
45g
PROTEIN
30g
CARBS
12g
FAT

Instructions

  1. Marinate 6 oz chicken breast in 1 tbsp olive oil, juice of 1/2 lemon, 1 tsp oregano, 1 minced garlic clove, salt and pepper for 30 minutes.
  2. Grill or pan-sear chicken until cooked through (165°F internal temp), about 5-6 minutes per side.
  3. Meanwhile, prepare base with 1/2 cup cooked quinoa and 1 cup chopped romaine lettuce.
  4. Top with sliced chicken, 1/4 cup diced cucumber, 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, 2 tbsp crumbled feta, and 5 kalamata olives.
  5. Drizzle with tzatziki sauce (mix 1/4 cup Greek yogurt with 1 tsp lemon juice, 1/4 tsp dill, and 1/4 tsp garlic powder).

Turkey Spinach Meatballs

390
CALORIES
38g
PROTEIN
25g
CARBS
14g
FAT

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine 1 lb lean ground turkey, 1 egg, 1/2 cup chopped spinach, 1/4 cup breadcrumbs, 2 minced garlic cloves, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp pepper.
  3. Form into 12 equal-sized meatballs and place on baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 18-20 minutes until internal temperature reaches 165°F.
  5. While meatballs bake, heat 1 cup marinara sauce in a skillet over medium heat.
  6. Add cooked meatballs to sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve over 1/2 cup whole wheat pasta or zucchini noodles, garnished with fresh basil.

Fudgy Protein Brownies

180
CALORIES
12g
PROTEIN
18g
CARBS
8g
FAT

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line an 8x8 baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt 1/4 cup almond butter and 2 tbsp coconut oil (about 30 seconds).
  3. Stir in 1/4 cup maple syrup, 1 tsp vanilla extract, and 1 egg until smooth.
  4. Add 1 scoop chocolate protein powder, 1/4 cup cocoa powder, 1/4 cup almond flour, and 1/2 tsp baking powder. Mix well.
  5. Fold in 2 tbsp dark chocolate chips (optional).
  6. Spread batter evenly in prepared pan and bake for 12-15 minutes (center should still be slightly soft).
  7. Let cool completely before cutting into 9 squares.
